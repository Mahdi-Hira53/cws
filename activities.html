Activities

Community World Centre has been providing events for the local community. Many generations have passed through our doors over the years, and we are proud to be one of the few community centres in London that offers activities for people of all ages, from 0-99+.

Care

CWS offers a variety of care activities for elderly persons around our community. Our care for individuals must be cheerful and enjoyable, and we encourage bringing people together. So, whether it's activities for the more energetic and able, a place to meet new people or a greater level of care for persons with dementia, we offer safe, friendly activities that engage and inspire everyone.
